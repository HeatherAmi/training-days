# Training Days

Full-day training content for live and recorded delivery. Each training day is designed to take a learner from zero to confident on a single topic in around six to seven hours of focused content.

If you are an MCT, a team lead delivering internal training, or a learner wanting a structured deep-dive, this is for you.

## Training days planned

### Dataverse in a Day
Tables, columns, relationships, business rules, security roles, and solutions. The foundation that everything else builds on.

### Canvas Apps in a Day
Power Fx, screens, galleries, forms, connectors, navigation, and publishing. End the day with a working app.

### Model-Driven Apps in a Day
Forms, views, dashboards, charts, site map design, business process flows, and a complete model-driven app.

### Power Automate in a Day
Cloud flows, expressions, triggers, actions, approvals, error handling, and connecting to Dataverse, SharePoint, and Teams.

### Security in a Day
Entra ID, security groups, licences, environment security, security roles, sharing apps, teams, and privileges. Everything you need to lock things down properly.

## Folder structure
